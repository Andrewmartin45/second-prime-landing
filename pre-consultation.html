“How is this different from working with my regular doctor?”

Your doctor is trained to detect and treat disease. We’re engineered for performance optimization. We use different testing protocols, different reference ranges, different goals, and a completely different implementation model. The targets are different — and so is everything else.

“Do I need to already be healthy to start?”

No. Most clients come to us having “done everything right” and still feeling like something is off. The assessment reveals what’s actually happening — and we build from there, wherever you are. The gap between your current state and your potential is often the biggest opportunity we have.

“What does the time commitment look like?”

Minimal ongoing friction. The assessment phase takes a few days. After that, implementation is designed to integrate into your life — not add complexity to it. We handle the logistics, the coordination, and the adjustments so you don’t have to. Your job is to execute what we put in front of you.

“Is this right for me if I’m already in good shape?”

Especially yes. The clients who get the most dramatic results are often those who are already disciplined and executing — but doing so without the right data. Precision transforms effort into results. Most high-performers are leaving significant performance on the table without knowing it.

“What’s the investment?”

Second Prime is a premium, high-touch service with limited availability. We cap new clients at 8 per month to maintain quality and depth of engagement. Pricing is discussed on the consultation call — what matters first is whether this is the right fit. If it is, everything else follows.
